About My Home Living

"My Home Living is an Amsterdam-based company that is dedicated to providing sustainable tiny home solutions for modern living. They specialize in designing and manufacturing tiny houses and offices that are not only beautiful but also environmentally friendly. The founders, who have a background in interior housing design and manufacturing, established the company during the COVID-19 pandemic as an opportunity to put their skills to use and create innovative and functional spaces that are built to last.

"My Home Living is a team of over 27 passionate individuals who are dedicated to creating the perfect tiny home for each and every customer. The team comprises of Dutch designer Cosmas Bronsgeest, development company owner Bart Berkhout, mechanical engineers, developers, electricians, maintenance staff, quality control, craftsmen and many more."

"Each team member brings their own unique set of skills and expertise to the table, working together to create innovative and functional spaces that are built to last. They are all driven by the common goal of creating the dream tiny home for every customer."
